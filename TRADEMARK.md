## Open Source vs. Trademarks

We are thrilled to offer the source code of Nebutra Sailor as open source under the AGPLv3 license to help developers build incredible products. However, our open-source license does **NOT** include the right to use our trademarks.

Open-source code and trademarks play different roles:
- The **code license** (AGPLv3) allows you to use, modify, and distribute the software itself.
- **Trademarks** protect the brand identity. They indicate the source of a product or service.

To protect users from confusion, we must control how our brand assets are used. Therefore, you cannot simply take the Nebutra Sailor project, run it, and call it "Nebutra Sailor" or use our logos.
