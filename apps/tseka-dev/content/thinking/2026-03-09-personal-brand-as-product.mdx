# Personal Brand as a Product

Most people treat their personal brand like a static resume. I treat mine like a SaaS product.

The insight is simple: a product that doesn't ship isn't a product — it's a prototype. The same is true for a personal brand that only updates when you remember to.

## The Compounding Math

One thoughtful public update per day. 365 days a year. That's 365 data points that tell the world who you are, what you think, and what you build.

This site runs on that principle. Every morning, my AI agent scans my work from the previous day and distills the high-signal moments into public updates.

## Why This Matters

Negotiating power comes from being known for something specific before the conversation starts.

## The Sailor Connection

This site is built with Nebutra Sailor — the open-source enterprise SaaS platform I'm building. Using your own tools in public is the best kind of dogfooding.

Build in public. Ship daily. Let it compound.
